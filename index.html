About Us

We are an educational institution founded April 1st 2003.

Our aim since inception has been to bring out the best in our society by focusing on children educational build up. We have a boarding facility to ease the stress of parents who desire their wards to come join us. We are not only good at impacting into our children the vitals to excel academically but also the importance of good character, love and faith in God. We have won lots of academic awards in time past and we look forward to winning more. We also participate in local sports ranging from basket ball, football, swimming and other recreational activities.
